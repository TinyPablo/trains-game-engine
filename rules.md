# OFFICIAL GAME LOGIC: TICKET TO RIDE EUROPE

## 1. Components & Setup
- **Wagons:** Each player starts with 45 wagons.
- **Stations:** Each player starts with 3 stations.
- **Starting Cards:** Each player receives 4 Wagon cards.
- **Starting Tickets:** Players receive 1 Long Route (blue) and 3 Regular Routes. They MUST keep at least 2.

## 2. Turn Actions (Strictly 1 per turn)
### A. Draw Wagon Cards
- Draw 2 cards total.
- If taking a face-up Locomotive: Counts as 2 cards (turn ends).
- If drawing from the deck: A Locomotive counts as 1 card (can draw a 2nd).
- 3-Locomotive Reset: If 3 face-up cards are Locomotives, discard all 5 and refresh.

### B. Claim a Route
- **Standard:** Pay matching color and number of cards.
- **Gray Routes:** Pay any color, but all cards must be the same color.
- **Ferries (Promy):** Indicated by Locomotive symbols. Player MUST play that many Locomotives + matching color cards for the rest.
- **Tunnels (Tunele):**
    1. Player plays required cards.
    2. Engine draws 3 cards from the deck.
    3. For every card drawn that matches the played color (or is a Locomotive), the player must pay 1 additional card of that color/Locomotive.
    4. If the player cannot/will not pay, the turn ends, and cards are returned to the player's hand.
- **Double Routes (2-3 Players):** Only one track of a double route can be used. Once taken, the other is closed.

### C. Draw Tickets
- Draw 3 cards from the Ticket deck.
- Must keep at least 1.

### D. Build a Station
- 1st Station: 1 card.
- 2nd Station: 2 cards (same color).
- 3rd Station: 3 cards (same color).

## 3. Scoring & End Game
- **Route Scoring:** 1:1, 2:2, 3:4, 4:7, 6:15, 8:21.
- **Unused Stations:** +4 points each.
- **European Express:** +10 points for the longest continuous path.
- **Tickets:** Successful = +Value; Failed = -Value.
- **Trigger:** When a player has 0, 1, or 2 wagons left, everyone (including that player) takes one last turn.
